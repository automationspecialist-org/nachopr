FAQs

What is Included in the Free Trial?

Unlimited searches, unlimited lists, unlimited social profiles.

Email lookups are not included in the trial, you will need to upgrade to view & export journalist emails.

How Do I Contact the Journalists?

You can click one button to save journalists to a list.

Once you have a list of journalists to contact, click the 'export' button to download a CSV file - this is a spreadsheet compatible with all CRM and email sending software such as LemList, Mailshake, Snov.io etc.

You can also open the spreadsheet and manually or have a virtual assistant email each journalist.
