Our Refund Policy

At NachoPR.com (operated by DF Digital Services Ltd), we want you to be completely satisfied with our services. We offer a straightforward refund policy to ensure your peace of mind.

Eligibility for Refunds

To be eligible for a full refund:

  • Request must be made within 7 days of purchase
  • Request must be sent via email to support@nachopr.com
  • Your account must be in good standing

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Email support@nachopr.com with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include your account email address
  3. Provide the date of purchase
  4. Brief reason for the refund request (optional but appreciated)

Refund Process

Once we receive your refund request:

  • We will acknowledge your request within 1 business day
  • If approved, refunds will be processed within 5-10 business days
  • The refund will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase

Exceptions

Please note:

  • Refund requests after the 7-day period will not be accepted
  • Any abuse of our refund policy may result in the termination of your account
  • Custom services or deliverables that have already been provided may not be eligible for refund

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@nachopr.com.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting to our website.
